WELCOME TO THE OFFICIAL PAGE OF JOSE TREZZA

Jose Trezza is an amateur pianist from Colombia, passionate about music, composition and music production. He currently divides his time among multiple activities, that mainly include a full time job as a Software Engeneer at Koombea, piano practice and doing experiments in his home studio, where he likes to uneleash his creativity. His work in the music ambit is available in his Youtube channel.

JOSE'S BIO

Jose's taste for music started at an early age, being attracted to musical instruments since he was very young, specially keyboard-like ones. After he finished highschool he founded a rock band along with some friends, a project that wouldn't last too much and that was dissolved just for some of their members (Jose included) to join a new project called Bareke Orquesta, a latin music orchestra that was widely recognized in the region and that, in 2012 achieved to win a "Congo de Oro", an important prize given by Carnaval de Barranquilla (Barranquillaâ€™s Carnival, in English), the organization that makes the most important carnival of Colombia, and one of the most important Carnivals in the world. Jose left the orchestra in 2014 to dedicate more time to his career as a software engineer, but his love for music has been there all this time, and that's the reason because he decided to come back to the piano and music in general, exited about sharing his love for music with all of you.
